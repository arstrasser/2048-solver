2048 with Genetic Algorithms

by Alex Strasser '19

   This is a demonstration of an AI agent that was trained using Genetic Algorithms to play the simple game of 2048. It was trained in python over the course of a couple weeks and played millions of games in order to learn strategies.

   The way genetic algorithms work is similar to populations of a species. A "generation" of neural network is randomized, and then they play a bunch of games. The best are selected and combined to create a new generation. This is repeated thousands of times until you are left with better generations that have learned how to play the game.


For more information, read the paper!
